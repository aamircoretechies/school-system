A school management system is a digital platform that organizes student information, academics, attendance, fees, staff workflows and communication in one place. CampusFlow is designed as a broader school operations platform, not only a student database.

It is designed for schools, institutes, coaching centers and education organizations that need a structured way to coordinate academic and administrative operations.

The platform can support admissions, student and staff records, attendance, timetables, examinations, assignments, fees, reporting and parent-school communication.

Yes. Role-based access can be configured so administrators, teachers and other users see the tools and information relevant to their responsibilities.

Implementation typically includes mapping the school structure, configuring roles and workflows, organizing existing records, and helping the team adopt the platform. Exact timelines depend on institutional requirements.

Multi-campus requirements can be discussed during discovery so the information structure, permissions and reporting model fit the organization.
